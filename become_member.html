How It Works

Becoming a DemocracyKit member is free. Here's how it works. Once you become a member, you will have access to the member directory, member discounts and member training sessions.

1

Create your account

Create your DemocracyKit account.




2

Complete your profile

Log in and update the four tabs in your profile: Contact Details, Profile Photo (or logo), Profile Details and About. Once you have completed your profile, the indicator on your account dashboard will read "Your Profile is 100% Complete".


3

Contribute

Contribute to DemocracyKit in some way. Anyone can contribute! Submit a video, article, library resource, volunteer some time or make a financial contribution to the Open Democracy Project.
